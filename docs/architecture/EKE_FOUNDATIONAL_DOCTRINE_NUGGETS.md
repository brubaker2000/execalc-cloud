## The 30 Nuggets

---

### PD-001 — Prime Directive Role
**Class:** Principle | **Activation:** Core

The Prime Directive determines whether an opportunity deserves to exist; it does not dictate execution.

---

### PD-002 — Balance Sheet Role
**Class:** Principle | **Activation:** Core

The balance sheet determines whether an opportunity strengthens or weakens the entity that pursues it.

---

### PD-003 — Market vs Structural Reality
**Class:** Principle | **Activation:** Structural

Supply/demand and risk/reward describe the opportunity; assets/liabilities describe what the opportunity does to the holder.

---

### PD-004 — Structural Value Equation
**Class:** Principle | **Activation:** Structural

Value emerges when favorable supply/demand and asymmetric risk/reward translate into net asset expansion rather than liability accumulation.

---

### PD-005 — Revenue Is Not Value
**Class:** Principle | **Activation:** Core

Revenue is a flow metric; equity is the objective. Revenue without asset capture is consumption, not value creation.

---

### PD-006 — Equity as the Scoreboard
**Class:** Principle | **Activation:** Core

The true measure of strategy is whether equity expands under stress conditions.

---

### PD-007 — Risk vs Liability Distinction
**Class:** Heuristic | **Activation:** Structural

Risk is probability; liability is exposure. Productive risk is acceptable only when liability is capped.

---

### PD-008 — Asymmetry Requirement
**Class:** Principle | **Activation:** Structural

Asymmetry exists only when downside is structurally limited and upside remains open.

---

### PD-009 — Liability Engineering Principle
**Class:** Heuristic | **Activation:** Structural

Opportunities are valid only when liabilities are pre-engineered and capped before commitment.

*Note: This extends the Assets/Liabilities Prime Directive lens with an operational rule. Candidate for canonical doctrine promotion.*

---

### PD-010 — Hidden Liability Multiplier
**Class:** Heuristic | **Activation:** Structural

Non-cash liabilities (complexity, dependency, reputation) are presumed double-weighted until explicitly controlled.

*Note: New formulation. Extends the liability lens by requiring non-financial liabilities to be surfaced and explicitly managed before discounting.*

---

### PD-011 — Scarcity Preservation Rule
**Class:** Principle | **Activation:** Structural

A profitable action that erodes structural scarcity destroys long-term value and must be rejected.

---

### PD-012 — Clarity as an Asset
**Class:** Principle | **Activation:** Core

Clarity is a structural asset that reduces liability, increases decision velocity, and compounds institutional equity.

*Note: Directly supports the Prime Directive two-tier model — clarity is a form of value, not merely a byproduct of it.*

---

### PD-013 — Clarity vs Certainty
**Class:** Principle | **Activation:** Core

Clarity reflects structural understanding; certainty reflects belief. Only clarity survives stress.

---

### PD-014 — Time Compression Principle
**Class:** Heuristic | **Activation:** Situational

Clarity compresses years of experience into present-moment decision filters.

*Trigger: activates when decision speed or time compression is a governing constraint.*

---

### PD-015 — Governance as a Clarity Engine
**Class:** Principle | **Activation:** Structural

Governance systems produce clarity by forcing structured evaluation of assets, liabilities, and asymmetry.

---

### PD-016 — Drift Prevention
**Class:** Principle | **Activation:** Core

Most strategic failure is drift; governed systems prevent drift by continuously enforcing structural evaluation.

---

### PD-017 — Directional Governance Principle
**Class:** Principle | **Activation:** Core

A governed system does not generate answers; it steers decisions toward durable equity expansion.

---

### PD-018 — Structured Aggression Doctrine
**Class:** Heuristic | **Activation:** Situational

Be aggressive in pursuing upside and disciplined in engineering downside.

*Trigger: activates during opportunity pursuit scenarios where operator may be over-cautious on upside or under-disciplined on downside.*

---

### PD-019 — Inversion of Failure
**Class:** Heuristic | **Activation:** Situational

Most people are cautious on upside and careless on downside; elite operators invert this behavior.

*Trigger: activates when operator is exhibiting asymmetric caution — conservative on upside, permissive on downside.*

---

### PD-020 — Productive vs Destructive Risk
**Class:** Principle | **Activation:** Structural

Risk is productive only when it expands assets under capped downside; otherwise it is destructive.

---

### PD-021 — Temporary Equity Compression Rule
**Class:** Heuristic | **Activation:** Situational

Short-term equity compression is justified only when it increases structural scarcity or defensible assets within defined limits.

*Trigger: activates when the scenario involves short-term sacrifice or investment against longer-term structural position.*

---

### PD-022 — Survivability Test
**Class:** Heuristic | **Activation:** Core

A decision is valid only if the worst-case outcome is survivable and does not permanently impair equity.

*Note: Concrete operational test for the Prime Directive. Candidate for canonical doctrine promotion. Should be enforced as a gate in the judgment pipeline.*

---

### PD-023 — Asset Recognition Rule
**Class:** Heuristic | **Activation:** Structural

If an asset cannot be clearly named, it should not be assumed to exist.

---

### PD-024 — Liability Recognition Rule
**Class:** Heuristic | **Activation:** Structural

If a liability cannot be capped or exited, it invalidates the opportunity.

---

### PD-025 — Clarity Compounding Effect
**Class:** Principle | **Activation:** Core

Clarity compounds by preventing catastrophic errors and improving capital allocation over time.

---

### PD-026 — Engine vs Governance
**Class:** Principle | **Activation:** Core

The LLM generates possibilities; governance determines direction and prevents drift.

*Note: Canonical identity statement candidate. Cleanly separates what the model does from what Execalc does.*

---

### PD-027 — Core System Identity
**Class:** Principle | **Activation:** Core

Execalc is not an answer engine; it is a governed judgment system designed to produce durable outcomes.

*Note: Strongest single-sentence system identity statement in the corpus. Candidate for canonical promotion to EXECALC_INVARIANTS.md.*

---

### PD-028 — Structural Filters Concept
**Class:** Principle | **Activation:** Core

Executive performance is driven more by filtering bad decisions than generating new ideas.

---

### PD-029 — Opportunity vs Suitability
**Class:** Heuristic | **Activation:** Structural

A strong opportunity can still be a poor decision if it weakens the holder's balance sheet.

---

### PD-030 — Ultimate Compression
**Class:** Principle | **Activation:** Core

No asset expansion, no value.
