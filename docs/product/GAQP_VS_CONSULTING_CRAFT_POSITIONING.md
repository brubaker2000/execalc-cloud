# GAQP vs Consulting Craft Positioning Note

## Purpose

This note clarifies the distinction between GAQP as a formal discipline and the existing qualitative synthesis work long performed by elite consulting firms.

It exists to answer a predictable question:

> **Is this just McKinsey or BCG in software form?**

---

## Short Answer

No.

Consulting firms have long practiced disciplined qualitative synthesis as a proprietary craft.

GAQP proposes something different:
- an explicit named standard
- a portable doctrine
- an auditable handling model for qualitative judgment
- a platform-native operational discipline

---

## What Consulting Craft Already Does Well

Top firms already:
- synthesize interviews, market signals, and internal narratives
- apply structured frameworks to business problems
- organize ambiguous situations into actionable judgment
- produce coherent strategic recommendations
- help clients think more clearly and rigorously

This is real value. It is not being dismissed.

---

## Where the Difference Begins

The difference is not that consultants do no qualitative synthesis.

The difference is that their synthesis is usually:
- firm-specific
- project-bound
- expert-mediated
- manually performed
- inconsistently preserved after the engagement
- not publicly formalized as a generally accepted standard

In other words, it is a craft practice, not a universal operating doctrine.

---

## What GAQP Adds

GAQP attempts to do for qualitative judgment what accounting standards did for quantitative reporting.

It introduces:
- named principles
- explicit treatment rules
- traceability requirements
- memory admission standards
- revision discipline
- decision-lineage expectations
- a portable language for rigor

This moves qualitative work from:
> elite artisanal practice

to:
> explicit governed discipline

---

## What Execalc Adds

Execalc makes that discipline executable.

It does not just provide expert labor. It provides a governed runtime in which:
- frameworks can be activated immediately
- qualitative inputs can be decomposed into atomic nuggets
- judgment can be made traceable and revisable
- memory can be preserved beyond a single engagement
- organizational thought can compound over time

---

## The Most Important Distinction

Consulting craft is usually:
> people performing disciplined qualitative synthesis for a client

Execalc + GAQP aims to become:
> a governed system that lets the organization perform, preserve, and reuse disciplined qualitative synthesis continuously

That is a different category.

---

## Proto-GAQP vs Explicit GAQP

A helpful framing:

- McKinsey / BCG / elite advisors have long practiced **proto-GAQP behavior**
- GAQP is the attempt to make that behavior **explicit, portable, standardized, and systematized**

So the relationship is not adversarial. It is evolutionary.

---

## Why This Matters Strategically

If the market believes Execalc is just "consulting software," then the category shrinks.

If the market understands that:
1. consulting firms practiced elements of qualitative rigor privately
2. GAQP formalizes qualitative rigor as a discipline
3. Execalc operationalizes that discipline as infrastructure

then the category expands dramatically.

---

## Positioning Thesis

McKinsey and BCG helped prove that disciplined qualitative synthesis has enormous value.

GAQP makes that rigor legible as a standard. Execalc makes it continuously executable inside the enterprise.

---

## One-Line Version

> Consulting craft performs qualitative synthesis. GAQP defines its discipline. Execalc turns that discipline into an operating system.
