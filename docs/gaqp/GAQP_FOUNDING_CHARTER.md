# GAQP Founding Charter & Reference Standard
**Status:** Canonized | **Version:** 1.0 | **Authority:** Execalc GAQP Standards Body

> "GAAP made capital allocation trustworthy at scale.
> GAQP makes executive judgment trustworthy at scale."

## I. The Founding Act

The founding move in every major discipline was not discovery but systematization.

**Euclid (~300 BCE):** Organized geometry into a deductive system with irreducible axioms.
Before Euclid: geometric knowledge. After Euclid: geometric science.
GAQP parallel: the 18 canonical claim types are the irreducible axioms of qualitative reasoning.

**Lavoisier (1743-1794):** Named oxygen, established conservation of mass, created nomenclature.
Before Lavoisier: alchemical practice. After Lavoisier: chemistry as a discipline.
GAQP parallel: the claim type taxonomy is governing nomenclature.

**Luca Pacioli (1447-1517):** Codified double-entry bookkeeping into a reproducible standard.
Before Pacioli: accounting as craft. After Pacioli: accounting as a governing system.
GAQP parallel: the Pacioli move for qualitative reasoning.

The GAQP founding act is systematization, not discovery.

## II. The Three Laws of GAQP

- Law I: Ungoverned AI predicts. Governed AI decides.
- Law II: Decision quality is bounded by the granularity of the units being analyzed.
- Law III: The ultimate value of governed AI is not speed — it is the perception of
  patterns previously invisible to human cognition.

## III. The Atomic Nugget

An atomic nugget is a governed claim: the smallest durable unit of qualitative
reasoning that can stand alone outside its source, be classified by type, be traced
to a source, be disputed or corroborated, be activated by context, and be composed
with other claims into larger reasoning structures.

Not every sentence is a nugget. A nugget is a sentence that survives governance.

The Compression Ratio: discourse compresses to atomic nuggets at approximately 44:1.
Organizations are storing and acting on 44x more noise than signal. GAQP isolates the signal.

## IV. The GAQP Constitution

1. AI without governance is a liability dressed as an asset.
2. The atomic nugget is the basic unit of organizational cognition.
3. Decision-cycle compression is the throughline of all technological progress.
4. Rebuild workflows from atomic units up, not patched over legacy architecture.
5. The Prime Directive asks the most important questions first.
6. Governed AI does not replace human judgment. It accelerates it to the natural limit.
7. Pattern visibility may be the ultimate competitive advantage of governed AI.
8. Every qualitative conclusion must be traceable, classifiable, bounded, and composable.
9. Organizational wisdom that cannot be encoded cannot be scaled or preserved.
10. GAQP is not a tool. It is the standard. Execalc is the platform that enforces it.
