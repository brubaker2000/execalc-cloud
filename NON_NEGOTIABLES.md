# NON_NEGOTIABLES.md
## Execalc — Build Non-Negotiables

These rules are binding. They are intended to prevent drift, shortcuts, and accidental collapse of the category vision.

---

## 1. Do Not Turn Execalc Into a Chatbot
Execalc is not a generic assistant, prompt wrapper, or conversational toy. The system must remain a governed intelligence platform.

---

## 2. The Model Is Not the Product
The LLM is an engine. The governance architecture is the product.

---

## 3. Governance Must Remain Visible
The Prime Directive, Core 7, Strategic Mesh, Support Stack, and Security Layer are not marketing language. They must remain visible in code, docs, and product structure.

---

## 4. Repo Truth Beats Chat Memory
If it matters, it belongs in the repo. The system must not depend on ephemeral chat state or undocumented assumptions.

---

## 5. Do Not Trade Architecture for Speed
No shortcuts that would later require a rewrite to reach enterprise-grade rigor.

---

## 6. Do Not Trade Governance for Convenience
If a feature weakens traceability, tenant safety, reasoning integrity, or auditability, it is the wrong feature.

---

## 7. No Cross-Tenant Bleed
Tenant isolation is architectural, not optional. Namespace enforcement must remain hard law.

---

## 8. Unknowns Must Stay Unknown
Do not hallucinate confidence. Missing inputs, uncertainty, and ambiguity must be represented explicitly.

---

## 9. Store Signal, Not Soup
Only structured, governable, strategically meaningful information should influence memory and runtime behavior.

---

## 10. Every Cognitive Feature Must Sit on Hardened Rails
No surface-level intelligence features without strong contracts, tests, persistence discipline, and auditability underneath.

---

## 11. Every Plumbing Feature Must Unlock Visible Intelligence
Infrastructure should serve cognition. Plumbing without product consequence is incomplete.

---

## 12. The Operator Comes First
The system exists to advance the operator's interests within governed constraints. The operator is not expected to be technical.

---

## 13. Explainability Is Mandatory
Important logic paths must be understandable in plain language by serious non-technical stakeholders.

---

## 14. Documentation Is Part of the Build
If an architectural choice is important, it must be documented. If it is not documented, it is not complete.

---

## 15. Enterprise-Grade Means Enterprise-Grade
Build for the standards required by serious institutions. No consumer-grade assumptions disguised as future plans.

---

## 16. Decision Artifacts Are Core
The system should produce structured executive judgment, not loose text output.

---

## 17. The Goal Is Decision-Cycle Compression
The organizing principle of the platform is to compress the decision cycle until the only remaining bottleneck is human judgment.

---

## 18. Preserve the Category Thesis
Everything should reinforce this truth:

> Execalc is governed intelligence infrastructure — the system that converts machine prediction into disciplined organizational judgment.
